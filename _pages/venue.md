## Koningshof Veldhoven

[NH Conference Centre Koningshof](https://www.nh-hotels.com/nl/hotel/nh-eindhoven-conference-centre-koningshof) sits just outside Veldhoven, near the city of Eindhoven, only a 15-minute drive from _Eindhoven Airport_ — making it highly accessible for both national and international guests. 
A former convent set in the lush woodland in the Brabantse Kempen, Koningshof offers modern hotel and conference facilities as well as a sports center which comes with a sauna, not to mention a swimming pool, gym and squash courts.

## How to reach Koningshof

- From **Schiphol Airport Amsterdam**: take the train to Eindhoven Centraal.
- From **Eindhoven Airport**: take Bus 20 - Bus 319, departs every 20 minutes (day-cards are available), by taxi: it's a 15 minute trip and is around €30.
- From **Eindhoven Centraal** train station: Take the Bus 19/319 to Koningshof. 
    A transport planner can be found at [9292.nl/en](https://9292.nl/en).
- By car: [Locht 117, 5504 RM, Veldhoven](https://www.google.com/maps/dir//51.3932901,5.3868587). Parking: onsite, outdoor. 7€ / 24 hours.
